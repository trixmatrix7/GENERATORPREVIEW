# 5×3 BONUS catalog: Wild Storm teardown, 3-scatter features, payout distribution

> Research sweep 2026-07-16 (round 2). Estimates marked.

## Executive summary

Wild Storm is a Stake-exclusive 5x3/11-line slot by Colorful Play (built on Stake Engine): its whole identity is one full-reel Tornado Wild that roams each free spin and carries a win-doubling multiplier (to 1,024x), with a flat-8-spins + "+1 per scatter" economy and a 3/4/5-scatter tier ladder (base / 8x-start / hidden 16x-start). Across the best 5x3 games, 3 scatters almost always buys ~8-10 spins of ONE legible transformation (collector wilds, expanding symbol, roaming wild reel, giant symbols, coin respins), triggering roughly 1-in-150 to 1-in-300 spins, with features carrying ~25-40% of total RTP and each scatter tier worth roughly 2.5-4x the previous (per bonus-buy pricing). For the slime-farm game, 7 candidate 3-scatter features are proposed that stay off the 4-scatter lane (sticky expanding plant wilds + growing multiplier): slime-jar collector spins, mutant-crop expanding symbol, roaming pig-nado wild reel, barn lock-and-respin, giant 3x3 livestock symbol, mystery slime stacks, and slime-elimination (symbol removal) spins.

## A. Wild Storm (Colorful Play / Stake Engine) — full teardown

**Who makes it:** Colorful Play, a small studio publishing exclusively through Stake's in-house **Stake Engine** platform — the game exists only on stake.com / stake.us ([Stake game page](https://stake.com/casino/games/colorfulplay-wild-storm)). Do not confuse with Boomerang/Yggdrasil's *Wild Storm Legionnaire* (432 ways) or *Valkyrie Wild Storm* — different games.

**Specs:** 5x3, **11 fixed paylines** (left-to-right, 3+ adjacent), **RTP 96.11%**, high volatility, **max win 15,000x**, bets 0.10–1,000.

**Base game:** Deliberately plain except one object: the **Tornado Wild Reel** — a full-reel (3-cell) wild that can land on any reel and substitutes for everything but scatter. With only 11 lines, one full wild reel touches nearly every line, so its arrival is always an event.

**Storm/wild mechanic (the core):** The Tornado carries a **multiplier that doubles every time the tornado participates in a winning connection** (1x→2x→4x→…), capped at **1,024x**. In free spins the tornado **teleports to a random reel every spin** — payline coverage reshuffles each spin, so an 8-spin bonus never plays the same twice. Doubling-on-win means the multiplier ladder is *earned*, not random — this is the exponential-tension hook.

**Free spins tiers (scatter ladder):**
- **3 scatters → Bonus:** 8 FS, one guaranteed moving Tornado Wild Reel from spin 1 (multiplier starts 1x).
- **4 scatters → Super Bonus:** 8 FS, Tornado starts at **8x**.
- **5 scatters → Hidden Bonus:** 8 FS, Tornado starts at **16x** (5-scatter-only secret tier).
- **Retrigger:** every scatter landed during FS adds **+1 spin** (drip-feed extension, not +10 blocks).

**Bonus buys (price ladder = value ladder):** Extra Chance 2x/spin (3x trigger odds), Tornado Spin 50x (one guaranteed tornado spin with random multiplier), Bonus 100x, Super Bonus 250x, Random Bonus 250x. Natural trigger rate is unpublished; from the 100x buy price and genre norms, **estimate ~1-in-160 to 1-in-250 spins** for the 3-scatter bonus.

**Why it works:** (1) ONE mechanic, fully legible — full-reel wild + doubling multiplier, no rule salad; (2) flat 8 spins is short enough that every spin matters, and +1-per-scatter creates constant micro-hope; (3) the doubling multiplier makes bonus quality *compound* — streamers chase the 64x/128x tornado clips (Trainwreckstv had a viral Wild Storm payout); (4) the 3/4/5 tier ladder reuses 100% of the same feature code with only a different starting multiplier — extremely cheap math tiering, the whole tier system is 3 constants (1x/8x/16x).

## C. Payout distribution & trigger-rate conventions

**RTP split (base vs features):**
- Nolimit City portfolio-wide: **base game 68.7% / bonuses 31.3%** of win value ([Slot Tracker](https://slottracker.com/no-limit-city/)).
- A genre survey puts typical **feature RTP contribution ~28% of total return**, with in-feature retrigger odds hovering near 18% ([thegameheaven](https://thegameheaven.com/scatter-games/math-behind-scatter-games-rtp-odds-and-payouts-explained/)).
- Working convention: **medium-vol games ≈ 70/30 base/feature; high-vol bonus-chasers push toward 55/45 or 50/50** (estimate — studios rarely publish per-game splits).

**3-scatter trigger frequency (community-tracked, large samples):**
- Big Bass Bonanza: **~1 in 150–183** spins ([Slot Tracker](https://slottracker.com/pragmatic/big-bass-bonanza/)).
- Book of Dead: **~1 in 174–192** (1/192.2 = 0.52% over 8.3M tracked spins) ([Slot Tracker](https://slottracker.com/play-n-go/book-of-dead/)).
- Wanted Dead or a Wild: **1 in 265.5** for any of the three bonuses ([Bigwinboard](https://www.bigwinboard.com/wanted-dead-or-a-wild-hacksaw-gaming-slot-review/)).
- Low-vol titles: as often as 1-in-50–100; heavy Megaways (Bonanza): ~1-in-450.
- **Convention: put your 3sc trigger at 1-in-150 to 1-in-250** for a high-ish-vol 5x3.

**Tier rarity (estimates from independent-reel math and buy pricing):** with per-reel scatter probability tuned for 3sc ≈ 1/200, **4sc lands roughly 8–15x rarer (~1-in-2,000–3,000)** and **5sc another ~30–80x rarer (~1-in-60,000–150,000)**. Hacksaw's 5-scatter Hidden Epic bonuses are deliberately non-buyable trophies at this rarity tier.

**How much bigger is a 4sc round? Bonus-buy prices are the industry's own EV statement:**
- Wanted D.o.a.W: 80x / 200x / 400x (2.5x, then 2x steps).
- Wild Storm: Bonus 100x → Super 250x (**2.5x**), Hidden not buyable.
- Nolimit typically 100x → 400–500x super tiers.
- **Convention: each scatter tier's average round value ≈ 2.5–4x the tier below** (buys price at ~average-round-EV/0.96, so a 100x buy ⇒ ~96x average round).

**Spins-awarded ladders in use:**
1. **Escalating spins:** 10/15/20 for 3/4/5sc (Big Bass; Pragmatic house style).
2. **Flat spins, escalating quality:** 10/10/10 with better guarantees per tier (Hacksaw; Book of Dead is flat 10 always).
3. **Flat short + drip retrigger:** 8 FS, +1 per scatter (Wild Storm).
4. **Few premium spins:** 5 FS compensated by giant-symbol density (Wolf Gold).

**Scatter direct-pay convention:** pay scatters as a win on top of the trigger — Book of Dead 2x/20x/200x, Sizzling Hot 10x/50x/200x for 3/4/5. Cheap RTP knob and softens dead bonuses.

**5-scatter secret-mode precedents:** Wild Storm Hidden Bonus (16x starting multiplier vs Super's 8x); Hacksaw Hidden Epic Bonuses (5sc only, 10–12 spins, everything guaranteed, progressive win bar, **cannot be bought**); Sweet Bonanza Super Scatter line. Making the 5sc tier unbuyable is a proven streamer-bait pattern.

## D. Seven candidate 3-scatter features for the slime-farm 10-line 5x3

Constraint recap: 4-scatter feature = **sticky expanding plant wilds + growing multiplier**. So the 3sc feature must avoid sticky wilds, expanding wilds, and multiplier ladders. All candidates run on the existing payline engine via the winEval façade; RTP dial noted per item. Target economy: trigger ~1-in-160–200, average round ~30–50x (so the 4sc round at ~2.5–3.5x that stays clearly premium).

**1. Slime Harvest Spins (collector) — top pick.** 10 FS. Slime-jar symbols land carrying coin values (0.5x–25x, rare 50x); the **Flying Pig** is a wild that, when it lands, vacuums every jar value on screen. Every 3rd pig = +5 spins. *Why 3sc-shaped:* pays in frequent small collects, no compounding. *Build:* jars are non-paying symbols with a value overlay; on pig landing, sum overlays and add to win — zero engine change, one post-eval pass. *RTP dial:* jar value table + pig reel weight. *Precedent:* Big Bass Bonanza (10 FS + collect).

**2. Mutant Crop Ceremony (expanding symbol).** 10 FS. Pre-bonus ceremony: a seed pod bursts and randomly picks one paying symbol as the **Mutant Crop**; after normal evaluation, if 3+ mutant symbols are anywhere on distinct reels they expand to full reels and pay **scatter-style across the 10 lines**. *3sc-shaped:* mostly small, occasional full-screen spike; volatility set by which symbols are in the draw pool and their weights. *Build:* post-eval second pass (count reels containing symbol X, pay lines × count) — pure façade math, plus one expand animation. *Precedent:* Book of Dead (10 FS + expanding symbol).

**3. Pig-nado (roaming full-reel wild).** 8 FS; a tornado-riding flying pig occupies one full reel and **jumps to a random reel every spin**; +1 spin per scatter. NO multiplier (that's the 4sc lane) — instead the pig reel pays a fixed 2x on wins it joins, or nothing. *Caution:* if your 4sc plant wilds also roam, this overlaps — differentiate by full-reel + moves-every-spin + never sticky, or drop this one. *Build:* force one reel to WILD before eval; pick reel index per spin. *Precedent:* Wild Storm's Tornado Reel.

**4. Barn Lock-and-Respin ("Slime Silo").** Not spins-with-lines at all: 3 respins on a 15-cell board; only slime-coin symbols (0.5x–10x) land and stick; counter resets to 3 on every new coin; fill all 15 = 100x jackpot on top. *3sc-shaped:* tight, capped, fast (60–90s), very controllable EV. *Build:* separate simple state machine, no payline eval needed at all. *RTP dial:* coin hit rate per cell per respin + value table. *Precedent:* Wolf Gold Money Respin / Fire in the Hole Lucky Wagon (simplified).

**5. Giant Livestock Spins.** 6 FS; reels 2–4 fuse into one **3x3 giant symbol** (slimed cow / flying pig / mutant pumpkin, weighted). *3sc-shaped:* few spins, each a binary thrill; average kept modest by giant-symbol weight table. *Build:* pick one symbol, write it to all 9 mid cells before eval — the payline engine sees 3 identical full reels; one big sprite on top. *Precedent:* Wolf Gold FS (5 spins + giant mid-reel symbol).

**6. Mystery Slime Stacks.** 10 FS; blank green **slime-blob symbols** land (often stacked); before evaluation all blobs on screen morph into the SAME randomly drawn paying symbol. *3sc-shaped:* constant near-miss texture, frequent mid wins, spikes only when blobs + premium draw align. *Build:* pre-eval substitution pass (replace blob ID with drawn symbol ID) — trivially simple; the reveal animation is the whole show. *RTP dial:* blob density on FS strips + morph-target weights. *Precedent:* Razor Shark mystery stacks / NetEnt mystery-symbol games.

**7. Slime-Off Elimination Spins.** 10 FS; every 3 spins, a slime wave dissolves the lowest remaining low-pay symbol off the reels (10s→Js→Qs), so spins 7–10 run on a premium-dense strip. *3sc-shaped:* back-loaded but bounded; no multipliers, no stickies. *Build:* 3–4 pre-authored FS reel-strip sets, swap on schedule — deterministic, easy to certify (fits your two-registry math-profile pattern). *Precedent:* symbol-upgrade/removal bonuses (Hacksaw's Cubes lineage, Play'n GO Moon Princess trance, simplified to strip swaps).

**Recommended pairing for contrast with the 4sc feature:** #1 (collector) or #4 (respin) — both pay through *instant cash values*, the exact opposite texture of the 4sc's slow-building sticky-wild/multiplier engine; #2 is the strongest if you want a ceremony moment. Ladder suggestion following section C: 3sc = candidate feature (~35–45x avg), 4sc = plant feature (~100–140x avg), 5sc = plant feature with head-start (2 plants pre-planted, multiplier starts elevated), unbuyable, à la Hacksaw Hidden Epic / Wild Storm Hidden.
