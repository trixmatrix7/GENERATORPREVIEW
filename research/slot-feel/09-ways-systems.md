# 09 — WAYS systems: rules, win read, drop feel

> Analyst-authored (companion to 08; cross-checked against reports 01/06).

## How ways pay

1. **No paths**: a win = the same symbol on CONSECUTIVE reels from the left,
   ANY row per reel. Ways = product of matching-symbol counts per reel
   (2×1×3 on three reels = 6 ways, each paying the 3-oak value).
2. Total ways = rows^reels (5×3 = 243, 5×5 = 3125 — our Vice Heat).
3. Wilds substitute per cell; scatter counts anywhere (identical to lines).
4. Per-symbol, not per-way, presentation: ALL cells of one symbol's win are
   one combo (the "fan"). Multiple symbols can win simultaneously.
5. Distinction — **scatter-pays** (Sweet Bonanza/Gates): not ways at all;
   8+ anywhere pays, no adjacency. Presentation differs again (pop/tumble).

## Win read without lines (the ways conventions)

- **No line is drawn.** The win reads through: dim board + the winning
  symbol GROUP lighting/animating together + one amount pop per symbol
  combo. (Our Vice ways-immersive = exactly this convention ✓.)
- Ways-count labels ("x243") appear in some games during the tally but most
  modern ways slots just show the amount per symbol group.
- Multiple same-symbol cells per reel = the fan; the presentation treats
  them as one organism (simultaneous pulse), never cell-by-cell.

## Drop feel for WAYS slots (vs lines)

- **Softer per-stop emphasis, faster overall cadence**: since any row
  connects, individual stops carry less read-tension; ways games run
  snappier spins and put the tension into scatter teases + features.
- **Higher hit rates** (60–75% with dust wins; Vice: 69.3%) — the drop
  rhythm can repeat faster without fatigue because wins punctuate often;
  the pay FLOOR question matters (our ≥0.077× floor).
- **Anticipation identical to lines** (scatter-driven), but the tease can be
  broader (whole-board tension) since no specific row must hit — our POV
  dolly fits ways especially well.
- Megaways adds the reel-height reveal as its own drop beat (n/a for us).

## Ways vs Lines — the comparison table

| | WAYS (Vice, 5×5/3125) | LINES (Crack Farm, 5×3/10) |
|---|---|---|
| Win definition | same symbol, consecutive reels, any row | same symbol along a fixed path |
| Win read | symbol-group fan + dim | line sweep through the path |
| Stop emphasis | soft, rhythm-driven | hard, each stop is a read event |
| Hit rate feel | high (60-75%), dust wins ok | moderate (25-45%), wins mean more |
| Presentation | group detonation (ways-immersive) | line-by-line cycle + comet |
| Sound accent | tumble/step ladders, feature risers | per-stop thunk ladder, line stingers |
| Max-win engine | ways mass × multipliers | premium 5-oak × feature multipliers |

Our two games sit exactly on this split by design: Vice = ways-immersive +
soft stops, Crack Farm = crack-slam stops + line comet. The pay-model feel
separation IS our differentiation between the two titles.
